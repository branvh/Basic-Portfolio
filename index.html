About Brandon

Brandon is a financial services professional with 9+ years of experience serving in various corporate strategy, business deelopment, and finance roles while working at firms such as General Motors, EY, JP Morgan and, currently, Price Waterhouse Coopers. Brandon works in the wildly fascinated world of payments (credit/debit/prepaid cards, wire payments, blockchain) and financial technology.

Brandon currently lives in Chicago, IL with his wife Jaime. Both Brandon and Jaime are University of Michigan alumni (Brandon loved it so much that he went there twice!)...Go Blue!

In the event that Brandon finds himself with spare time (as unlikely as that may be) you can probably find him on a golfcourse nearby. Brandon and Jaime love cooking, particularly trying cheeses (aside fromt the smelly ones like blue) and wines from Trader Joe's.
